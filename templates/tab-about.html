Fonte da Vida

Fonte da Vida is a joint partnership between Equip Mozambique and the local mozambican churches, principally Peniel International.

The goal of the application is to provide resources to the local believers in a language and method they can understand. Any church is able to upload resources on the app provided they follow the guidelines.

If you want to upload your resources to benefit the Mozambican people, please click the link above or e-mail us for more information. We are always looking for more organizations to partner with and would love to hear from you.
